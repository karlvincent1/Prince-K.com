Casts


Kousei Arima

Kousei Arima is the main protagonist of the series Your Lie in April. He is the youngest winner of the Maihou Piano Competition. He became a pianist thanks to the encouragements of his mother's close friend. He tends to be oblivious of what happens around him, to not notice the feelings from the other people such as the rivalry of his competitor pianists, or Tsubaki Sawabe's crush on him.

Kaori Miyazono

Kaori Miyazono is the main female protagonist of Your Lie in April. She is a gifted violinist attending the same school as Kousei Arima. She went to see him for the first time at a recital when she was still a child, and since then, she became passionate for the violin. She is a weak woman who went under several surgeries, after her hospitalization, she even physically changed, such as her skin fading a bit, and she missed school several times.

Details



Tsubaki Sawabe

Tsubaki is Kousei Arima's childhood friend who has always gone to his performances and cheered him on. Tsubaki would often invite Kousei to play outside but due to his practice time with his mother, Kousei could rarely participate. Being with Kousei for a long time, Tsubaki gradually took on the role of an "older sister". Kousei was that "hopeless little brother she had to take care of". She would often prepare gifts for Kousei and cheer him up when he was sad, such as when his mother abandoned his pet cat he had found and he couldn't find him. Rather than being interested in music, Tsubaki was always athletic.
